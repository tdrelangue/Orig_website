Independent audits for accessible, usable, and sustainable digital products

Technical expertise in WCAG compliance, cognitive load reduction, and performance optimization.

What I do

  • Accessibility audits

    WCAG 2.2 AA compliance testing with screen readers and keyboard navigation patterns.

  • Cognitive usability audits

    Interface evaluation focused on cognitive load, information architecture, and comprehension patterns.

  • Digital sobriety audits

    Performance measurement, page weight analysis, and eco-design compliance reviews.

How I work

  1. Initial assessment Review current state, identify scope, and define success criteria.
  2. Technical audit Execute automated and manual testing across defined checkpoints.
  3. Detailed reporting Document findings with severity levels, code examples, and remediation steps.
  4. Implementation support Provide technical guidance during fix implementation and validation.
  5. Verification Re-test resolved issues and confirm compliance or performance targets.

Who it's for

This service is designed for organizations with specific compliance, usability, or sustainability requirements.

  • Digital teams in regulated sectors requiring WCAG 2.2 AA compliance
  • Product teams targeting users with cognitive or visual impairments
  • Engineering and IT departments implementing accessibility standards
  • Marketing and communications teams ensuring inclusive content delivery
  • Organizations with carbon reduction or eco-design commitments
  • Internal compliance officers seeking independent validation

Not suitable for general UX optimization or conversion rate projects without clear accessibility or sustainability objectives.
